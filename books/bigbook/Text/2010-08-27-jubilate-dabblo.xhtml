Jubilate Dabblo

This week in my cupboard at The Dabbler, a bit of blatant puffery for the recording I made—with Germander Speedwell—of Christopher Smart's bonkers epic Jubilate Agno. I am well aware that Hooting Yard readers have learned the habit of listening to it daily, setting aside the three hours necessary to do so. Now you will, I hope, be joined by the extended readership of The Dabbler.
