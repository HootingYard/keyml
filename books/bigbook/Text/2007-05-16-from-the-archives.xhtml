From The Archives

That sounds like a job for a factotum. [That would be us —Hooting Yard Archivists] Is it possible to be one's own factotum? While I ponder such an imponderable, I may post a few orts and scantlings from the Archive here, in a whimsical manner. This, for example, which first appeared on Thursday 18th March 2004.
