The Right Sort Of Stoning

Is stoning ever justified?

“It depends what sort of stoning and what circumstances,” he replies.

– From an interview with Dr Muhammad Abdul Bari, leader of the Muslim Council of Britain, in the Daily Telegraph, 10th November 1007… sorry, 2007.
