Oh Tilly! Such a smashing gal
Nowadays she's my penpal
We swap letters ‘cross oceans wide
Oh Tilly! Tilly will abide

Abide with me
Abide with Ned

Soon enough the pair did part

Some influenced by old H. Bosch
