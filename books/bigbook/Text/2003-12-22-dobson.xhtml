Dobson

How are we to get the measure of Dobson? We do not know his first name. We know neither the year of his birth, nor that of his death. (We do know that he is buried near his favourite lighthouse.) Virtually every last scrap of his work has been destroyed. All that remains is a short play, The Glue In The Palace Was Rarefied; The Putty Was Dreadful.
