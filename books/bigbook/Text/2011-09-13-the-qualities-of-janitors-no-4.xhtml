Among Sam's other attributes was the gift of divination. He was the primitive man, a part of nature's self, and he looked upon the truth unblinded, undimmed by the veil of knowledge formalized. In this respect he was absolutely uncanny, a person to be discussed and analyzed in remote rooms or upon quiet walks.
